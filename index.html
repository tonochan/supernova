How to play

  1. H+H →He

    Tap a group of 2+ touching tiles of the same color to fuse them. Atomic numbers add up.

  2. Li+Be →N

    Numbers don't need to match — color is all that matters, and fused tiles keep it.

  3. Ca+C →Fe

    Reach Fe (26) and the tile goes supernova ✦. Novas only fuse with other novas.

  4. Md+Ho →●

    Push past Og (118) and novas collapse into a black hole. Holes devour each other, too.

  5. When nothing can fuse, the sky is full — game over. Can you light up all 118 elements on the periodic table?
